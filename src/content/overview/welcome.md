### Introduction

Most YSWSs are challenge-based; ship ABC, and we’ll send you XYZ prize, where the participant goes away, learns independently, and uses the Hack Club community to share ideas and get support, building a “toolbox” of skills to tackle even larger projects.

But what if you’re a beginner, and your “toolbox” of skills is relatively empty?

This is where workshops and clubs come in! As a club leader, you run workshops and offer in-person support, helping to guide people through their first projects and build the skills they’ll need to go forth and create even larger, more complex projects!

Create a beginner-friendly workshop for your club, share it with the world on the Guides site, and we’ll provide the funding for you to run your workshop like a YSWS for one run-through! We’ll provide the resources and mentoring to help you create a high-quality workshop.

For exceptionally high-quality and unique submissions, we may invite you to run your workshop as part of a larger, official Hack Club YSWS. Whether you're looking to create an activity for your club or want to run a YSWS, here’s your chance!

### What we’re looking for

We’re looking for workshops that fill a gap in our existing YSWS offerings, that teach skills and how to use frameworks, languages, techniques, etc, that we aren’t already teaching and do so creatively!

Workshops should be beginner-friendly and teach attendees everything, from start to submission, step-by-step, in 1-3 hours.

### What we are offering

Through Project YSWS, we’re offering a unique opportunity to share your work with the wider Hack Club community, and gain valuable experience in designing beginner-friendly experiences and projects!

We’re offering to everyone who creates a workshop:

- Advice and Mentorship.
- An opportunity to submit to guides.hackclub.com, and share your workshop with the world.
- Funding for one in-person run-through of your workshop at the standard $5/hour rate with your club.
- [Not confirmed/discussed with team] A Fillout form for handling project submissions for your club runthrough

For a select number of exceptional submissions

- An invitation to turn your workshop into a Club-friendly, official YSWS for the whole of Hack Club.
- An assigned Club YSWS mentor to guide you through the process.
- Support with YSWS branding and artwork.
- An Airtable Base and Fillout access for managing global submissions to your YSWS.
