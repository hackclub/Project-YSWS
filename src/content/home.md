We'll be helping guide you every step of the way along the path of building your very own workshops!
After hosting workshops such as Boba Drops, FuseRing, and Swirl ... what would you like next?

You can start off by reading through the documentation available on this website,
in the overview section.
It was created by the team, and significantly increases your chances of having a successful workshop!
Then, once you feel ready with an idea of what you'd like to create, [apply](/overview/apply) using the form.

Once approved, create a guide and game plan, and then lead the program at your own club! 
Hack Club will fund it at a maximum of $5 USD per hour spent per participant. 
If it goes successfully, and you're interested, we will help you scale it as a real Hack Club program for other clubs around the world to enjoy as well!

One of the most important parts of this process is not being afraid to ask questions. All of us "mentors" were new to this as well in the recent past, and we \[add\] so don't hesitate to reach out if you need any help along the way!

Whether you're a club leader looking to get your members shipping more projects, interested in running a custom workshop funded by Hack Club, or are looking to benefit high schoolers globally, this is your chance!
