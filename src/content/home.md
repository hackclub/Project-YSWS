We'll guide you every step of the way on your journey to building your very own workshops!

After hosting workshops like Boba Drops, FuseRing, and Swirl, what would you like to lead next?

Start by reading through the documentation available in the [Overview](/overview) section of this website. It designed to help familiarize you with the process and as a result increases your chances of hosting a successful workshop. Once you have a solid idea of what you’d like to create, [apply](/overview/apply) using the form!

Once approved, you'll create a guide and game plan, then lead the program at your own club! Hack Club will fund your initiative for up to $5 USD per hour per participant. If your workshop is successful, and you're interested in expanding it, we can help you scale it into a real Hack Club program for other clubs around the world to enjoy as well!

One of the most important parts of this process is not being afraid to ask questions. All the mentors were also new to this when creating their own workshops, and they will be more than happy to help you. So don't hesitate to reach out if you need help along the way!

Whether you're a club leader aiming to help your members ship more projects, interested in running a custom workshop funded by Hack Club, or looking to benefit high schoolers globally, this is your chance!
