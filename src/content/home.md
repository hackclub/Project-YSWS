We'll guide you every step of the way on your journey to building your very own workshops!

After hosting workshops like [Boba Drops](https://boba.hackclub.com), [Fusering](https://fusering.hackclub.com), and [Swirl](https://swirl.hackclub.com), what would you like to lead next?

Start by reading through the advice availabe in the [overview](/overview) section, written by current YSWS organisers! They're designed to help you get familiar with the process, and help you to run a successful workshop! Once you have a clear idea of what you'd like to create, and what we're looking for, [apply](/overview/apply) using the form!

Once approved, you'll create a guide and game plan, then lead the program at your own club! Hack Club will fund your initiative for up to a maximum of $5 USD per hour per participant. If your workshop is successful, and you're interested in expanding it, we may invite you to scale it up into an official Hack Club YSWS for other clubs around the world to enjoy!

The most important thing to keep in mind is to not be afraid to ask questions. All of the Project YSWS mentors have been where you are now, and are more than happy to help! So please don't hesitate to reach out if you need help or advice along the way!

Whether you're a club leader aiming to help your members ship more projects, interested in running a custom workshop funded by Hack Club, or looking to benefit high schoolers globally, **this is your chance!**
